Break the Emotional Gridlock — Starting with One Conversation

Far from home, when academic setbacks, career walls, or culture clashes spike your anxiety, therapy can feel "too heavy" while coping alone feels "too exhausting." CNPAF offers the lightest, fastest non-clinical navigation there is. Three simple steps to your breakthrough roadmap:

1

Reach Out

Send a short email to gethelp@cnpaf.org, or submit the request form at the bottom of this page.

2

Rapid Screening (Completely Free)

3

Get Your Remedy

We do more than listen — you leave with a tailored "Two-Week Action Plan": concrete tools and direction to break through.

A Support Network for Every Life Stage

Many psychological struggles are really unsolved practical problems. So our support goes straight at the real-world pain points.

Stage I

Navigation Point
Upfront Psychological Navigation & Resource Matching

Every visitor starts here. Through bilingual conversation we map your situation and distinguish everyday emotional stress from conditions that need professional clinical care.

Stage II

Buffer Zone
Structured Non-clinical Support (Core Service)

We bind emotional support tightly to practical problem-solving. Anxious about academics? We help you process the feelings — and coach you through the appeal email. Facing workplace barriers? We bring culturally informed strategies.

Stage III / IV

Medical Safety Net
Clinical Intervention & Professional Referral

When assessment shows a clear clinical need, we use our academic network to refer you seamlessly to licensed psychiatrists with Asian cultural backgrounds — ensuring the safest possible care.

华人心理援助基金会 CHINESE PSYCHOLOGICAL ASSISTANCE FOUNDATION

The Chinese Psychological Assistance Foundation (CNPAF) is a nonprofit dedicated to culturally attuned, mentorship-driven support for overseas Chinese communities. We assume every visitor is "not ill" — filling the systemic gap between struggling alone and clinical treatment. We are proudly included in the UN DESA Civil Society Database.
