We are dedicated to professional psychological support and humanistic care for overseas Chinese communities. Adapting to a new environment is a demanding journey, and the gap between Eastern and Western cultures can bring profound stress. But you do not have to face it alone — we are here to build the bridge. Recognized globally for our cultural-led community care, CNPAF is proudly profiled in the UN DESA Civil Society Database.

Our Manifesto

Building a Psychology Standard of Our Own

Psychology has long been a social science led by Western scholars and built around Western populations. We founded CNPAF to establish a new standard of psychological support, assessment, and care that truly serves Chinese, Asian, and other minority communities.

Stage I–IV Program (Full-Cycle Psychological Navigation)

Culturally attuned, de-pathologized non-clinical support. Our four-stage system spans upfront psychological navigation, resource matching, deep cultural adaptation, and clinical referral — a real, safe buffer zone for overseas Chinese.

The Chinese Psychological Assistance Foundation (CNPAF) is a nonprofit dedicated to culturally attuned, mentorship-driven support for overseas Chinese communities. We assume every visitor is "not ill" — filling the systemic gap between struggling alone and clinical treatment. We are proudly included in the UN DESA Civil Society Database.
