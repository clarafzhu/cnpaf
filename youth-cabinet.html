Global Youth Leadership

CNPAF Youth Cabinet

A global youth-empowerment and leadership development program. We shift the question from "what have you learned" to "what can you create" — a living laboratory that mirrors how modern society works.

The Cabinet in Action

Joining the Youth Cabinet means stepping out of the ivory tower into real social issues. Browse the list to explore the change our young members are driving around the world.

Global Awards Authorization

Backed by Premier International Honors

As an officially certified partner, we mentor members toward two youth honors that carry real weight with international admissions officers. More than awards — they are a passport for ambitious young leaders worldwide.

The Duke of Edinburgh's International Award

Often called the "Nobel Prize of the youth world," it carries exceptional weight in applications to top Commonwealth, European, and American universities — a mark of outstanding character, resilience, and social responsibility.

US Congressional Award

The highest honor the US Congress confers on young people, and America's only federal-level youth award. The medal is national recognition — and a powerful door-opener to top US universities.

Model Parliament & Bicameral Governance

No make-believe simulations. Modeled on modern parliamentary democracy, our rigorous bicameral structure gives ambitious youth a real platform to serve, lead, and safely learn from mistakes.

The Upper House

Strategic decision-making

Composed of regional Branch Leaders. Sets the annual strategic direction, reviews major cross-regional projects, and decides budgets and external affairs.

Builds: strategic thinking / resource integration / negotiation

The Lower House

Execution & operations

Composed of core operations officers from each Branch. Proposes motions, executes projects, manages members, and channels community feedback.

Builds: project management / teamwork / crisis handling

华人心理援助基金会 CHINESE PSYCHOLOGICAL ASSISTANCE FOUNDATION

The Chinese Psychological Assistance Foundation (CNPAF) is a nonprofit dedicated to culturally attuned, mentorship-driven support for overseas Chinese communities. We assume every visitor is "not ill" — filling the systemic gap between struggling alone and clinical treatment. We are proudly included in the UN DESA Civil Society Database.
