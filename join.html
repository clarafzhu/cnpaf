Get Involved

Join us in reshaping the standards of our field. Beyond non-clinical support that crosses cultural divides, we are building bridges of youth empowerment and intergenerational understanding through real action.

Every contribution you make here — volunteering on the front lines, conducting rigorous research behind the scenes, or keeping operations running — injects real strength into the community. Whether you are a seasoned professional or a passionate young leader, CNPAF welcomes you.

华人心理援助基金会 CHINESE PSYCHOLOGICAL ASSISTANCE FOUNDATION

The Chinese Psychological Assistance Foundation (CNPAF) is a nonprofit dedicated to culturally attuned, mentorship-driven support for overseas Chinese communities. We assume every visitor is "not ill" — filling the systemic gap between struggling alone and clinical treatment. We are proudly included in the UN DESA Civil Society Database.
